Approach


I'm a creative person from Wroclaw. I work above all types of websites, in terms of design, interactivity and advertising.
I focus on one thing: a creative delivery which works.
I always carry out projects with care and accuracy to the smallest details that are the key to success.
The customer is put in first place. He has a vision and I try to implement it, the projects are adapted to the requirements and needs of the client.

How I work?

1. IDEA

Creating an idea for a project that has a chance to break out and show your own skills

2. PLANNING

Lay out the plan of action on the project and the stages of creating individual project elements in the approximate time.

3. SYSTEMATIZED WORK

Systematic work on the project according to the plan of action and achievement of the intended elements. After completing the plan, catching and correcting mistakes in delayed backup time.

4. FINISH

Finish of the project, publication and description of the created project.
